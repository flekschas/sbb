Heat map of RNA expressions

Color Range

0% 100% 50%

Heat map modus lets you color the current illustration according to the units RNA expression.

Select genes from the search panel in the bottom right and the units will be colored relatively to the minimum and maximum RNA expression value of your selection.
The current minimum and maximum expression value can be found above the gene selection.

The RNA expression data is provided by the RNA Seq Atlas1.

For further information please refer to the about page.
