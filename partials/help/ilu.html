Actions

HighlightSingle Click

By clicking on a unit it will be highlighted as well as its corresponding tag in the list of units on the right.

Information / ZoomDouble Click

A double click on a unit will open a pop-up containing information about this unit as well as the possibility to zoom in if available.

Infos

A unit is an area of interest within the illustration. This can be for example an organ, an important structure within an organ, a cell or a cellular component.
Units can be identified by hovering with the mouse's cursors. If an area turns yellow it represents a unit.
