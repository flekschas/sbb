The search distinguishes between views / illustrations and units. The first, bold line of a hit states its name. The second and third line list alternative hits of the same entity (e.g. human and mouse podocyte)

Hits are sorted by relevance and the first, bold line always links to the best hit among all alternative (which are also ranked by relevance).
