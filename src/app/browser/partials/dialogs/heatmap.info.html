Heat map of RNA expressions

Color Range

0% 100% 50%

Heat map functionality lets you color the human body illustration according to the relative RNA abundance.

Select genes from the search panel in the bottom right and anatomical entities will be colored relatively to the minimum and maximum RNA expression value of your selection.
The minimum and maximum expression value can be found above the gene selection.

The RNA expression data is provided by RNA Seq Atlas2 and Human BodyMap 2.02. Raw counts are normalised to transcripts per millions.

Genes can be de- and selected using your mouse or keyboard only. A click on the top right "x" button will close the whole heat map modus.

The coloured entities are shown together with a stacked bar chart in the entities panel. When multiple genes are selected a click on a single bar within the bar chart will highlight this single. To deselect the gene simply click again on the bar. Same can be done by clicking on the genes label.
