Info

A zoom-in level is a new illustration that shows a more detailed illustration of a specific area whereas a zoom-out level shows a broader view.

Development

This button opens a menu with all developmental stages of the current view.

Species

This button opens a menu to switch the current species. At the moment of this writing we support homo sapiens and mus musculus

In higher zoom levels the views do often look the same but the units are mapped to different ontological terms.

Gender

This button will switch the gender.
